Blob Video Test Page

How to test blob video downloads:
  1. Click "Create Blob Video" below to generate a blob URL video
  2. Click the extension icon to scan for videos
  3. Try downloading the blob video
  4. Check that the downloaded file has a reasonable size (several MB, not 110 bytes)
  5. Verify the downloaded video plays correctly
